Our Policy

At our fitness studio, we strive to provide a safe and welcoming environment for all of our customers. To ensure that everyone has an enjoyable experience, we have established some policies that we ask all of our customers to follow.

Health and safety
Rules and restrictions
  • If you have any injuries or medical conditions, please inform your instructor before the class starts. They will provide you with modifications or alternative exercises as needed.

Classes
Refunds
  • We require a minimum of 24 hours' notice for cancellation of a class.
  • Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds or makeup classes for missed classes.
  • We offer refunds for unused classes within 30 days of purchase.
