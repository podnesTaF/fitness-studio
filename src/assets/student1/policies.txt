Fitness Studio Policies

Welcome to our fitness studio! To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all members, we have established the following policies. By using our facilities and participating in our classes or programs, you agree to comply with these policies.

1. Membership and Registration

All participants must have a valid membership or register for classes/programs before attending.
Memberships are non-transferable and non-refundable unless otherwise specified.
Participants must provide accurate and up-to-date information during registration.
2. Health and Safety

Participants are responsible for assessing their own health and fitness levels before engaging in any physical activities. It is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before starting an exercise program.
If you have any pre-existing medical conditions, injuries, or concerns, please inform our staff and seek appropriate medical advice.
Participants must follow safety instructions provided by our trainers and staff.
Proper workout attire and footwear must be worn at all times.
3. Conduct and Etiquette

Treat all staff members and fellow participants with respect and courtesy.
Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language and behavior.
Cleanliness is important; please wipe down equipment after use and dispose of any trash properly.
Refrain from excessive noise or disruptive behavior that may interfere with others' workouts.
Do not take photos or videos without prior consent from the management.
4. Class Reservations and Cancellations

Class reservations may be required and can be made online, in-person, or through our designated booking system.
Late arrivals may not be admitted to the class, as it may disrupt the session.
Cancellations should be made within the designated timeframe to avoid penalties or loss of class credits. Check our cancellation policy for details.
5. Payment and Fees

Membership fees, class fees, and any additional charges must be paid in accordance with our payment terms.
Late payments may result in suspension of membership or denial of services.
We reserve the right to revise our fees and pricing structure with prior notice.
6. Facility Usage

Follow our facility guidelines and rules at all times.
Properly use and handle equipment and facilities to avoid damage.
Report any equipment malfunction or maintenance issues to our staff immediately.
7. Privacy and Data Protection

We value your privacy and handle personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws. Review our privacy policy for more details.
8. Changes to Policies

We reserve the right to modify or update these policies as needed. Any changes will be communicated to members via our website, email, or other appropriate means.
Disclaimer:
Participating in physical activities and using our facilities involve inherent risks. By using our services, you acknowledge and accept these risks. We recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before starting any exercise program.
